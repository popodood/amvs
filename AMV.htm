Adopt Me Values bot demonstrates everything shown in ?help command. You may use these commands as you would like within your server as long as what you are doing does not break discord's ToS.

Adopt Me Values DOES NOT store GuildIDs and UserIDs unless you break ToS or exploit Adopt Me Values, you'll get blacklisted from using Adopt Values SmoBlox which makes us store your GuildID of your server or UserID depending on who broke ToS. If you've been blacklisted and would like to delete your data, contact @SmoBlox. You can also join our support servers "SmoBlox Squad" or "Adopt Me Pet Values" to request data removal.

Underage Users

The use of the Bot is not permitted for minors under the age of 13, or under the age of legal consent for their country. This is in compliance with the Discord Terms of Service. No information will be knowingly stored from an underage user. If it is found out that a user is underage we will take all necessary action to delete the stored data.

Questions

If you have any questions or are concerned about what data might be being stored from your account contact @SmoBlox, or join either "SmoBlox Squad" or "Adopt Me Pet Values". For more information check the Discord Terms Of Service.
